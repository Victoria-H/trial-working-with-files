Wrap-Up

In this lesson you learned how to read a delimited file and how to define the related schema. You also learned how to apply simple transformations on the input data and write them into an output file.

 

tRowGenerator

Imagine you wish to have a column in your output files with a random date in 2016.
What expression would you use to do this?
HINT : Open the expression builder.

  • TalendDate.getRandomDate("2016-01-01","2016-12-31")
  • String.getRandomDate("2016-01-01","2016-12-31")
  • TalendDate.getDate("2016")
