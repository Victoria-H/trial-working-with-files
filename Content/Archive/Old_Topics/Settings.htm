Settings

Overview

You will display and edit the settings for Talend Studio.You will also understand how to use the help functionality.

Studio Settings

  1. To edit the Studio settings click Windowthen click Preferences:

  2. Click General and and on the right side you see the list of available perspectives. Make the the default perspective and click the button Make Defaultand then click OK:

  3. You see the active perspective on the top right and can also select it from there:

  4. You can always reset the perspective, click on Window then click Reset Perspective and then click OK:

  5. The active perspective is also part of the menu, click Window then click Perspective to pick Integration from a list of perspectives:

Help

  1. To display the Help you click Help and click Help Contents:

  2. The "Contents" including a search item will be displayed:

  3. The typical " F1" help button shows related help text. Select a Job in the "Job Designer" or click a component in a Job and press the " F1" function key and the " Related Topics" will be displayed on the right hand to the Palette:
