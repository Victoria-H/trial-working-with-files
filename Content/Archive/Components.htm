Settings

Overview

You display and edit the settings for Talend Studio and edit the Layout for the component Palette. You will understand how to use the help

Studio Settings

  1. To edit the Studio settings click "Window" then click "Preferences":

  1. A simple example is to make a certain perspective "default", like "Integration" for DI. Click "General" and "Perspective" on the right side select "Integration" from the list then click "Make Default" and then click "OK":

  1. You see the active perspective on the top right and can also select it from there:

  1. Customizing your perspective may take some time and you might like to save this for the next project. Click "Window" and click "Save Perspective As...":

  1. Enter "DI_BASIC_Training" for the "Name" and click "OK":

  1. Your active perspective becomes "DI_BASIC_Training":

  1. You can always reset the perspective, click on "Window" then click "Reset Perspective" and then click "OK":

  1. Now the new perspective is also part of the menu, click "Window" then click "Integration" to set it to the default for the "Integrationperspective":

  1. To check if the new perspective is part of the list click "Window" then click "Preferences":

 

Project Settings

  1. Project settings will be started with a click on "File" and click "Edit Project properties":

  1. When you select "Job Settings" and then "Use Project Settings" you can import or export project settings. Here you see a list which may yours at the end of the DI BASIC training:

  1. The content of the Palette can also be set. You select "Designer" and click "Palette Settings":

  1. Select "Talend MDM" out of "Show" and use the arrow to the left between the two table to move it to the first column "Hide" and click "OK":

Note: this allows you to customise the project , here the content of the palette regarding to your active project.
