Using tFilterRow_test

Overview

To become familiar with further components in Talend Studio you will now use the component "tFilterRow" to filter a certain" state.

Duplicate Job

  1. Right-click on the Job "FilterStates" and click "Duplicate":

  1. Name the new Job "FilterStates_tFilterRow" and click "OK":

Edit

Job

  1. Double-click "tMap" and delete the filter ""CA".equals(row2.StateCode)" then click "OK":

  1. Enter a new component "tFilterRow" to the Job:

  2. Right-click "tMap" and select "Row" then select "*New Output*(Main)" and connect it to "tFilterRow":

  1. Enter "Filter" for the "new Output name": and click "OK":

  1. Double-click "tMap" and you will see the additional schema for the connection "Filter" which is empty. You need to drag & drop the fields from "row1" to the output schema "Filter" and then enter "StringHandling.UPCASE(row1.State)" for the uppercase ouput:

  1. Drag & drop "StateName" from "row2" to the schema "Filter" and place it at the end then click "OK":

  1. Click "Yes" for "Would you like to propagate changes?":

  1. Enter a new component "tFileOutputDelimeted" :

  1. Select "tFileOutputDelimeted" and click "Component" then enter "C:/StudentFiles/tFilterRow_out.csv" in "File Name":

  1. Right-click "tFilterRow" and click "Row" then click "Filter" and connect it to "tFileOutputDelimited":

  1. Click "tFilterRow" again and click "Component" then click the green "+" and select "State" from the drop-down list for the "Input Column" and enter "CA" for the "Value":

Run

Job

  1. Click "Run" and you see the 27 rows for "CA" are filtered:

  1. Open the file "C:/StudentFiles/tFilterRow_out.csv" and verify the output:

Rejected

  1. Enter a new component "tFileOutputDelimted" to the Job to write the data not matching the filter to another output. Select the new component and click ""View" to enter "Rejected" into the field "Labelformat":

  1. Right-click "tFilterRow" and click "Row" then click "Reject" and connect it to "Rejected":

  1. Select the component" Rejected" and click "Component" to enter "C:/StudentFiles/Rejected_out.csv" for the "File Name":

  1. Click "Run" and you will see, that the data not matching the filter "CA" will be written to "Rejected":
