Using tReplicate

Overview

  1. To become familiar with further components in Talend Studio you will now use the component "tReplicate" to filter on several  states in one Job.

Enter tReplicate

  1. Right-click under "tMap" and enter the new component "tReplicate":

  1. Right-click "tMap" and click "Row" then click "Filter" and connect it to "tReplicate":

  1. Right-click "tReplicate" and click "Row" then click "Main" and connect it to "State_CA":

  1. Click "Yes" to answer to "Would you like to propagate changes?":

  1. Right-click again on "tReplicate" and click "Row" then click "Main" and connect it to "State_NY":

  1. Click "Yes" to answer to "Would you like to propagate changes?":

  1. Right-click again on "tReplicate" and click "Row" then click "Main" and connect it to "State_AK":

  1. Click "Yes" to answer to "Would you like to propagate changes?":

  1. Click the yellow triangle on the top-right of "State_CA" to display the "Warning" which indicates that the input schema is different to the output schema:

  1. Double-click the output component "State_CA" then click "Component" and click the button "Sync columns". Repeat this for "State_NY" and for the output component "State_AK" please:
